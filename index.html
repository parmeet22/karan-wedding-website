Events

30th March

Welcome Poolside Prem Rang 9 AM

Dive into a poolside celebration of love at 'Poolside Prem Rang'! Don your most vibrant and colorful casuals as we gather by the shimmering waters for an evening of joy and laughter. Bahrana Sahib 3PM

The Sindhi Behraana ceremony is a significant pre-wedding ritual in the Sindhi community, typically held a few days before the wedding day. Behrana Sahib consists of jyot, sugan candy, phota, fruits, clove and akho. Behind is an earthen pot with a coconut in it, covered with cloth, flowers, leaves and the idol of God Jhulelal

Sangeet-e-ring 7PM

Join us for a night of music, dance, and celebration at our Sangeet and Ring Ceremony. Embrace the sparkle and shine of the occasion by dressing in glittery and shimmery Indowestern attire, as we groove to the beats of love and laughter. Let's create unforgettable memories together as we embark on this joyous journey towards a lifetime of happiness. Your presence will make our celebration complete. See you there!

After party 11PM

Amsterdam theme based Haldi 9AM

Get ready to immerse yourself in the enchanting vibe of Amsterdam at our Haldi ceremony Amidst the Dutch-inspired decor, we'll celebrate with the warmth of turmeric and the sweetness of blessings. Dhikh Sehra bandi 1PM

Experience the timeless tradition of Dhik Sehrabandi as we embark on a journey of love and unity. Join us for this auspicious ceremony where the groom receives blessings and adorns the Sehra, symbolizing the beginning of a new chapter. Dress in your finest traditional attire, and witness the beauty and grace of this cherished tradition. Your presence will add joy and warmth to our special day.
